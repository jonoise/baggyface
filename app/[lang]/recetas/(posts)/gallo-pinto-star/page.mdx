# Gallo Pinto Star Recipe

## Ingredients

- Arroz
- Frijoles
- Cebolla
- Chile dulce
- Culantro
- Ajo
- Aceite de oliva
- Sal
- Salsa Lizano

## Instructions

1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add the chopped onion, sweet pepper, and garlic to the pan. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
4. Add the cooked rice and beans to the pan. Mix well.
5. Season with salt and a generous amount of Lizano sauce.
6. Cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is heated through.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!

## Serving Suggestions

- Gallo Pinto is traditionally served with scrambled eggs, fried plantains, and sour cream.

Enjoy your Gallo Pinto Star!
